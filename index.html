WHAT WE DO?

At Manassa, we are dedicated to improving business and personal security through our comprehensive range of products and services. From advanced ERP and POS solutions to custom software development, we offer tailored solutions to meet your unique needs.
Our expert teams excel in network system installation, implementing state-of-the-art CCTV camera systems, and integrating fire alarm and extinguishing systems to ensure optimal security.
Furthermore, we provide top-notch consultations and training services in the ICT field, equipping individuals and organizations with the skills and knowledge they need to thrive in the digital landscape.

Programming

We offer software development for wide range of applications starting from web, mobile, and desktop, to IoT, and embeded applications. We provide tailored solutions to meet the unique needs of our clients.

ERP

We provide ERP implementation, support, and hosting services for top-notch systems like ERPNext and Odoo. Streamline your business processes and optimize efficiency with our comprehensive ERP solutions.

Sales Systems & POS

We provide set of POS and sales management systems for various industries such as restaurants, cafes, and retail shops, accompanied by professional support to ensuring that our clients receive the highest level of assistance and expertise.

Networking

Our expert teams are proficient in designing, implementing, and installing extensive network systems, encompassing in-site networking, VPN, MAN, Telecom, and more.

Security & CCTV Systems

We offer design and installation for set of security systems, including CCTV cameras, alarm systems, and fire extinguishing solutions. We are officially retilers for respected brands like Dahua and Hikvision.

Consultation & Training

In order to help others achieve their goals and drive success, we are offering an expert guidance and customized training programs to empower them with the knowledge and skills needed in ICT field.
